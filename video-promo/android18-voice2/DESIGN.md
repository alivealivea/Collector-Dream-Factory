# Collector Dream Factory — Android 18 Voice 2 Promo

## Overview

This version keeps the premium editorial warmth of the website but relaxes the pacing to match a natural Thai performance at roughly thirty-eight seconds. The website remains the star, while Android 18 acts like a deadpan host who has seen one too many people underestimate collectible scale.

## Colors

- **Canvas Ivory**: `#F7F3EE`
- **Gallery White**: `#FFFFFF`
- **Lifted Surface**: `#EFE7DC`
- **Primary Ink**: `#17130F`
- **Secondary Copy**: `#6D6258`
- **Quiet Copy**: `#9A8F84`
- **Collector Gold**: `#B9822B`
- **Gold Highlight**: `#C4933D`
- **Measurement Cyan**: `#3AAFA9`
- **Warm Shadow**: `#483218`

## Typography

- **Thai Primary**: `Sukhumvit Set`, with `IBM Plex Sans Thai` and `Leelawadee UI` as close fallbacks.
- **Display / Brand Close**: keep the same Thai stack for consistency in this cut.
- Headline captions use 72–94 px at 700–800 weight. Supporting labels use 24–32 px.

## Motion

- Keep scene entrances punchy, but let the middle of each beat breathe longer.
- Cursor clicks, pill selection, and scale growth should land on spoken emphasis.
- Use anime impact accents sparingly: one strong life-size punchline, one playful understanding tag, one warm close.

## Components

- **Website Phone Stage**: portrait crop of captured pages inside a rounded device window.
- **Android 18 Cutout Host**: transparent figure with slight body bob, eye-line accents, and mouth flap.
- **Scale Selector**: four pill buttons with cursor ripple and a cyan measurement indicator.
- **Collector Card Rail**: Akaza, Goku, and R2-D2 cards moving horizontally at different depths.
- **Order Flow Grid**: four large numbered cards with quick completion clicks.
- **Warm Brand Close**: full-height proof image, URL, and final collector punchline.

## What Not To Do

- Do not introduce neon or cyberpunk colors.
- Do not cover the website with excessive text.
- Do not make the pacing frantic just to fill time.
- Do not use stiff default Thai UI typography.
