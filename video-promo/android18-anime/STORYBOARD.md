## Beat 1 — Website Inspection (0.00–5.90)

Android 18 drops beside a tilted mobile website panel as if she has been assigned to inspect it. A cursor clicks the scale-preview area; the panel responds with a gold ripple while her mouth flap and eye accent react to the voice.

Transition: gold diagonal wipe revealing the scale controls.

## Beat 2 — Scale Escalation (5.90–11.40)

The Room Scale Preview becomes the stage. Buttons 30, 60, and 100 cm punch in sequentially with click rings. Android 18 grows at each click while labels change from “วางโต๊ะได้” to “เริ่มเบียดทีวี” to “คนในบ้านเริ่มถาม”.

Transition: cyan measurement line expands vertically into the next beat.

## Beat 3 — 170 cm Punchline (11.40–14.30)

The 170 cm button is clicked. A record scratch freezes the website, speed lines explode outward, and Android 18 fills the room. The line “ฉันอยู่บ้านนี้” stamps onto screen like an anime title card.

Transition: fast whip-pan into the collection rail.

## Beat 4 — Collector Choice (14.30–21.35)

Akaza, Goku, and R2-D2 cards slide past like character-selection screens. Android 18 watches from the foreground while the final card reveals the caption “ระดับความเข้าใจของคนที่บ้าน”.

Transition: warm blur-through into the order flow.

## Beat 5 — Ordering Is Easier (21.35–26.30)

The four real website steps appear as large numbered cards: send image, choose size, estimate, deposit. A cursor completes them rapidly; the last card is interrupted by the dry punchline about explaining the purchase.

Transition: circular gold iris into the final proof frame.

## Beat 6 — Brand Close (26.30–30.00)

The real life-size Android 18 photo fills the upper frame. Brand name, URL, and closing line settle below. Android 18’s cutout gives one final tiny confident nod as the music resolves.

Final fade to warm ivory.
