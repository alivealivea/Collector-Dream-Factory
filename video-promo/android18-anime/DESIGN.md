# Collector Dream Factory — Android 18 Anime Promo

## Overview

The promo inherits the website’s warm editorial gallery identity, using ivory surfaces, dark brown typography, restrained gold accents, and cyan only for interactive measurements. Website screenshots remain recognizable while anime motion language appears as a comedic overlay: speed lines, reaction stamps, cursor impacts, and sharp typographic timing.

## Colors

- **Canvas Ivory**: `#F7F3EE`
- **Gallery White**: `#FFFFFF`
- **Lifted Surface**: `#EFE7DC`
- **Primary Ink**: `#17130F`
- **Secondary Copy**: `#6D6258`
- **Quiet Copy**: `#9A8F84`
- **Collector Gold**: `#B9822B`
- **Gold Highlight**: `#C4933D`
- **Measurement Cyan**: `#3AAFA9`
- **Warm Shadow**: `#483218`

## Typography

- **Display**: Fraunces 500 for the brand name and editorial closing statement.
- **Thai / UI**: IBM Plex Sans Thai 400–800 for narration captions, UI labels, and punchlines.
- Headline captions use 72–94 px at 800 weight. Supporting labels use 24–32 px.

## Elevation

Website panels use white or ivory surfaces, 26–34 px rounded corners, thin warm borders, and broad soft shadows. Anime overlays use hard-edged ink shapes and gold impact rings so they feel deliberately separate from the premium UI.

## Components

- **Website Phone Stage**: portrait crop of captured pages inside a rounded device window.
- **Android 18 Cutout Host**: transparent figure with slight body bob, eye-line accents, mouth flap, and a warm drop shadow.
- **Scale Selector**: four pill buttons with cursor ripple and a cyan measurement indicator.
- **Collector Card Rail**: Akaza, Goku, and R2-D2 cards moving horizontally at different depths.
- **Kinetic Thai Captions**: conversational phrase groups with one emphasized word per joke.
- **Anime Impact Layer**: radial speed lines, reaction stamps, and a single record-scratch freeze.

## Do’s and Don’ts

### Do

- Keep the website legible and visually dominant.
- Use gold for brand actions and cyan for measurement interactions.
- Let comedy come from timing and realistic collector behavior.
- Use fast anime accents only around punchlines.

### Don’t

- Do not turn the whole piece into neon cyberpunk.
- Do not cover Android 18’s face with captions.
- Do not use generic AI gradients or excessive glow.
- Do not imitate a named voice actor; use an original anime-style performance.
