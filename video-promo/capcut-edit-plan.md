# 🎬 Collector Dream Factory: CapCut Edit Plan

A detailed guide to editing the 30-second promo video inside **CapCut** (Mobile or Desktop).

---

## 🎵 1. Music & Sound Design

### Background Music (BGM)
* **Track Type:** Cinematic Lofi, Emotional Ambient Piano, or Warm Acoustic Guitar.
* **Search Terms (CapCut Library):** "Cinematic Lofi Piano", "Warm Aesthetic Acoustic", "Inspiring Ambient".
* **Audio Flow:** 
  * **0:00 - 0:15:** Low/soft volume (around -15dB) to let the voiceover command attention.
  * **0:15 - 0:27:** Subtle build-up with light strings/beats (increase volume slightly to -12dB).
  * **0:27 - 0:30:** Fade out naturally.

### Sound Effects (SFX)
* **0:00:** Soft outdoor wind/ambience.
* **0:03 & 0:05:** Quick "digital click" or "bubble pop" SFX when tapping selectors in screen recordings.
* **0:08:** Light "woosh" transition SFX.
* **0:27:** Soft chime or metallic ring for the logo reveal.

---

## 🎨 2. Color Grading & Filters
To achieve a premium, warm, and cinematic collector look rather than a generic or AI-generated appearance:

* **Apply Filter:** "Warm & Golden" or "Autumn Glow" at **45% opacity**.
* **Manual Adjustments:**
  * **Contrast:** `+8` (gives depth to shadows)
  * **Saturation:** `-6` (reduces neon colors, making materials look grounded)
  * **Warmth / Temp:** `+12` (emphasizes cozy bedroom/living room home vibes)
  * **Highlights:** `-10` (prevents harsh digital exposure)
  * **Shadows:** `-5` (creates deep blacks)
  * **Vignette:** `+8` (darkens borders slightly, guiding attention to the center)

---

## 🔤 3. Typography & Captions
* **Font Choice:** Clean, readable Thai font. (e.g., "Pridi", "Kanit", "Mitr", or standard CapCut fonts like "K2D" or "Bai Jamjuree").
* **Style:** White body text, with **gold color** (`#D4AF37` / HSL `36, 65%, 50%`) for keywords (e.g. *ตัวละครที่คุณรัก*, *Masterpiece*, *ชิ้นเดียวในโลก*).
* **Text Animation:** 
  * In: **"Fade In"** + **"Tracking"** (letters slow-expanding).
  * Out: **"Fade Out"** (0.2s).

---

## 🎞️ 4. Transitions & Video Effects
* **Shots Transitions:**
  * Keep transitions clean. Use simple **"Fade to Black"** (0.2s) or **"Camera Blur Zoom"** (0.3s).
  * Do not use high-intensity glitch, flash, or geometric screen-wipe transitions.
* **Keyframing:**
  * For still photos (Android 18, Akaza, R2-D2), add a slow keyframed zoom (Scale 100% at start of shot ➡️ Scale 105% at end of shot) to create a slow-moving cinematic drone look.
